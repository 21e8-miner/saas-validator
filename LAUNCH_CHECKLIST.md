## Potential Exit Strategies

1. **Acquisition Targets**:
   - No-code platforms (Bubble, Webflow)
   - Startup tools (Gumroad, ConvertKit)
   - Accelerators (YC, Techstars)
   - Financial software (Stripe, Brex)

2. **Valuation Multiples**:
   - SaaS companies typically sell for 5-10x ARR
   - At $1M ARR: $5-10M valuation
   - At $3M ARR: $15-30M valuation

3. **Bootstrap Strategy**:
   - Run profitably indefinitely
   - 95% margins = owner salary + reinvestment
   - Lifestyle business generating $500K-1M/year profit

## Emergency Pivots

If initial traction is low, consider:

1. **Niche Down**: Focus on specific vertical (e.g., "AI SaaS Validator")
2. **Expand Use Case**: Add product validation, not just SaaS
3. **B2B Pivot**: Sell to accelerators/VCs as white-label tool
4. **Freemium Adjustment**: More/less features in free tier
5. **Pricing Change**: Test $19, $39, $49 price points
